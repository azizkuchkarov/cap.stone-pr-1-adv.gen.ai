# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## What is this support system?
This is an AI-powered customer support system that answers questions based on available documentation and helps users create support tickets when additional assistance is needed.

## What documents does the system use to answer questions?
The system uses multiple internal documents, including:
- Technical manuals
- Company policies and procedures
- Frequently asked questions (FAQ)

Answers are generated only when relevant information is found in these documents.

## How can I contact customer support?
You can contact customer support by email or phone. If your issue requires further assistance, you may also request the system to create a support ticket on your behalf.

## What should I do if I cannot find an answer in the documentation?
If the system cannot find a confident answer in the documentation, it will inform you and suggest creating a support ticket for further investigation.

## How do I create a support ticket?
You can ask the assistant to create a support ticket. To do so, you will need to provide:
- Your name
- Your email address
- A short summary of the issue
- A detailed description of the problem

## What types of issues should be reported via support tickets?
Support tickets should be created for issues such as:
- Problems not covered in the documentation
- Unclear or missing information
- Suspected errors in the documentation
- Complex or case-specific technical issues

## How long does it take to receive a response to a support ticket?
Support tickets are typically reviewed within one to two business days, depending on the complexity of the issue.

## Does the system always provide an answer?
No. The system only provides answers when sufficient information is found in the documentation. If the answer is uncertain or missing, the system avoids guessing and recommends creating a support ticket.

## Does the system keep conversation history?
Yes. The system maintains recent conversation history to provide context-aware and consistent responses during the chat session.

## Is my personal information stored permanently?
Personal information such as name and email is used only for support purposes, including ticket creation, and is handled according to company policies.
